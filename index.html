About

Divyanchal is a 17-year-old independent Punjabi artist, writer, and music producer based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Working primarily in hip-hop and pop, he focuses on building a distinct sonic identity through honest writing, self-produced music, and long-term creative vision. His work blends raw emotion with modern sounds, creating a world listeners can connect with and grow alongside.
